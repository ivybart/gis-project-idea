GIS PROJECT IDEAS

Everything geospatial information

The following are some of the ideas on how to apply GIS solutions to various problems.
The ideas apply knowledge learnt in class to help solve a variety of issues, in sectors such as agriculture,
business and transport.

Flood Mapping

The project involves mapping the regions that flood in Tana River and therefore help identify suitable control measures. It applies microwave remote sensing i.e processing and analysis or RADAR images.

Suitability Analysis

The idea here is to establish areas that are most suitable for growing kiwi fruits in Kenya. It uses various datasets such as river maps , soil maps , rainfall distribution as well as land use maps.

Network Analysis

This project is intended to create a path for hikers in Karura. It chooses the optimal and safest path for this particular group whether, hiking or cycling.
